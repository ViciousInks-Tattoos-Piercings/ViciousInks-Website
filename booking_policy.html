Booking & Payment Policy

Please read and understand our booking and payment rules before scheduling your appointment.

  1. 1. Payment Methods

    • Card payments and EFT (bank transfer) only.
    • Cash payments will be charged 20% extra

  2. 2. Booking Requirements

    • All appointments require either:
      • R50 non-refundable booking fee — secures your appointment, but the full price is still due at the appointment.
      • Full payment upfront (no booking fee is required.), OR
    • No booking will be confirmed without one of the above.

  3. 3. No Walk-ins

    • All sessions are by appointment only.
    • Walk-ins will be charged 20% extra

  4. 4. Session Rules

    • Please do not bring more than 2 guests with you. No children are allowed
    • No alcohol before or during your session.
    • Arrive on time — lateness without communication may result in cancellation and loss of your booking fee.

  5. 5. Additional Information

    • Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the area being tattooed or pierced.
    • Bring a snack and/or water for long sessions to stay energized and hydrated.
